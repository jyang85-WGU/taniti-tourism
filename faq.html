Frequently Asked Questions

What type of power outlets are used on Taniti?

Taniti uses 120-volt outlets, the same as the United States.

What is the legal drinking age?

The drinking age on Taniti is 18. Alcohol may not be served or sold between midnight and 9:00 a.m.

What languages are spoken on Taniti?

Many younger Tanitians speak fluent English. However, English is less commonly spoken in rural areas, especially by older residents.

Is Taniti safe for tourists?

Violent crime is very rare on Taniti. However, as tourism increases there have been occasional reports of petty crimes such as pickpocketing.

What currency is used on Taniti?

Taniti uses the U.S. dollar. Many businesses also accept euros and yen. Several banks offer currency exchange and many locations accept major credit cards.

What healthcare services are available?

The island has one hospital and several clinics. The hospital has many multilingual employees.

Are there holidays that affect tourism?

Taniti celebrates many national holidays. Some attractions and restaurants may be closed during these times, so visitors should plan ahead.
