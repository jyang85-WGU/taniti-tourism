Lodging Options

Taniti offers a wide variety of lodging options for visitors, ranging from budget-friendly stays to resort-style accommodations.

Hostels

Budget-conscious travelers can choose inexpensive hostel options while still staying close to key parts of the island.

Hotels and Bed & Breakfasts

Visitors can stay in family-owned hotels or growing bed and breakfast options that offer a more local experience.

Resort Stay

Taniti also features one large four-star resort for travelers seeking a more full-service vacation experience.

All types of lodging are strictly regulated and regularly inspected by the Tanitian government to help ensure quality and safety.
