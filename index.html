Discover Taniti

Taniti is a small tropical island in the Pacific with sandy and rocky beaches, lush rainforests, a safe harbor, and a mountainous interior with an active volcano. Visitors can enjoy outdoor adventure, local culture, sightseeing, and relaxing island hospitality.

Why Visit Taniti?

Natural Beauty

Enjoy white sandy beaches, tropical rainforest trails, scenic island tours, and views of Taniti’s active volcano.

Unique Experiences

Visitors can snorkel, fish, zip-line, take helicopter rides, explore museums, and enjoy local nightlife.
